# Welcome to Hagog International Ltd. 🚲

### A legacy of excellence in mobility and recreation.

Since 1976, Hagog International has been a leader in the Israeli cycling and wheeled products industry. From our humble beginnings as a family shop, we have grown into a brand synonymous with reliability, strength, and uncompromising quality.

We specialize in the import and distribution of premium bicycles, tricycles, scooters, and go-karts, always prioritizing the highest finishing standards. Safety is our core value; all our products meet the strictest Israeli and European standards, manufactured in advanced facilities under rigorous international quality control.
